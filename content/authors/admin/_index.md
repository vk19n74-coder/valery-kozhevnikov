Valery Kozhevnikov is an Associate Professor in the Department of Applied Sciences at Northumbria University, Newcastle upon Tyne. He leads a research group specialising in luminescent coordination chemistry, with expertise spanning phosphorescent transition metal complexes, OLED materials, luminescent solar concentrators (LSCs), and bioorthogonal 1,2,4-triazine chemistry for applications in chemical biology and nuclear medicine pretargeting.

He holds an h-index of 34 with approximately 86 peer-reviewed publications and 6 granted patents, and has held EPSRC grants as Principal Investigator. The group collaborates with industry partners including Merck on the development of OLED emitter materials.
