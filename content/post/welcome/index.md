Welcome to the new website of the Kozhevnikov Lab at Northumbria University. 

We are a research group in the Department of Applied Sciences specialising in luminescent coordination chemistry. Our work spans phosphorescent metal complexes for OLEDs, 1,2,4-triazine bioorthogonal reagents, luminescent solar concentrators, and TADF emitters.

Please explore the site to learn more about our research, team, and publications.
